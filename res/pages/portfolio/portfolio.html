AnamNex: AI-Powered Medical SaaS for Emergency Physicians

Web App Laravel Nginx JavaScript AI ECG Analysis Medical Imaging AI Healthcare SaaS

AnamNex is a complete medical SaaS platform developed to support emergency physicians (plantonistas) using artificial intelligence. Built with Laravel PHP framework on Nginx server, the platform provides automated ECG interpretation from technical files (.mat, .hea) and images, plus AI-powered analysis of wounds, skin lesions, and dermatological conditions. The system processes cardiac signals to detect arrhythmias and generates structured clinical reports in under 30 seconds, helping physicians make faster, more accurate diagnostic decisions during emergency shifts.
