Wesley Mod Store

WordPress-based e-commerce store for digital products and gaming mods.

Project Overview

Wesley Mod Store is a WordPress e-commerce platform built for selling digital products and game mods. The focus was on creating a clean, fast, and conversion-optimized storefront with smooth navigation, product clarity, and a modern gaming-oriented design.

Visit Website

Your Role

I developed the WordPress-based storefront with WooCommerce integration, focusing on product layout, responsive design, and improving the overall user experience for digital product sales.

Features

  • Custom WordPress WooCommerce setup
  • Digital product and mod delivery system
  • Responsive modern storefront UI
  • Optimized product browsing experience
  • Fast-loading category and product pages

Tech Stack

WordPress WooCommerce HTML CSS JavaScript Elementor

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was structuring a clean digital product store that remains fast and easy to navigate. I optimized layout hierarchy, reduced clutter in product pages, and improved mobile responsiveness for better conversions.

Results & Impact

The redesigned store improved usability and product visibility, resulting in a smoother customer journey and better engagement across product pages.
