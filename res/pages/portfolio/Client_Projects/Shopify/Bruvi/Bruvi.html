Bruvi

Premium single-serve coffee system reimagined for taste and sustainability.

Project Overview

Bruvi is a premium coffee brand reinventing the single-serve experience with B-Pods that deliver superior taste and are more eco-friendly. I built a high-conversion Shopify Plus store with integrated subscription functionality, custom product bundling, and a refined UI that reflects their modern, sustainable brand identity.

Visit Website

Your Role

I led the Shopify store development — including custom theme architecture, subscription integration, product bundling logic, checkout optimization, and third-party app integrations to enhance the customer journey from discovery to repeat purchase.

Features

  • Custom Shopify Plus theme with premium brand aesthetics
  • Recurring subscription system for B-Pod deliveries
  • Product bundling (starter kits, variety packs, machine + pods)
  • Seamless checkout experience with one-click reorder
  • Interactive product comparison tool for coffee machines
  • Sustainability-focused UI highlighting eco-friendly initiatives

Tech Stack

Shopify Plus Liquid JavaScript HTML CSS Subscription API GraphQL

Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was implementing a flexible subscription system that allowed customers to customize delivery frequency, pod quantities, and flavors. I leveraged Shopify's Subscription API and built custom Liquid components to manage customer preferences. Another challenge was optimizing the bundle builder for speed — solved by asynchronous loading and efficient state management.

Results & Impact

The Bruvi store launched with a 30% increase in subscription sign-ups compared to their previous platform. Customer retention improved due to the seamless reorder system, and the site's modern design reinforced Bruvi's position as a premium, innovative coffee brand.

Future Improvements

Future updates include AI-powered flavor recommendations based on purchase history, a loyalty rewards program, and integration with smart coffee machines for automated reordering.
