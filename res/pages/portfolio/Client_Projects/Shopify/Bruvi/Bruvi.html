Bruvi is built on Shopify Plus with a fully customized Liquid theme. The store leverages Shopify's Subscription API for recurring B-Pod deliveries, GraphQL for efficient data queries, and custom JavaScript for interactive product bundling. The front-end uses HTML/CSS for responsive layouts, ensuring a premium coffee shopping experience across all devices.

Bruvi is a premium coffee brand reinventing the single-serve experience with B-Pods that deliver superior taste and are more eco-friendly. I built a high-conversion Shopify Plus store with integrated subscription functionality, custom product bundling, and a refined UI that reflects their modern, sustainable brand identity.

The client needed a robust e-commerce solution that could handle recurring subscriptions, product bundles, and an interactive comparison tool for their coffee machines. I built a fully customized Shopify Plus store from the ground up — including custom theme architecture, subscription integration, product bundling logic, checkout optimization, and third-party app integrations to enhance the customer journey.

The main challenge was implementing a flexible subscription system that allowed customers to customize delivery frequency, pod quantities, and flavors. I leveraged Shopify's Subscription API and built custom Liquid components to manage customer preferences. Another challenge was optimizing the bundle builder for speed — solved by asynchronous loading and efficient state management.

After deploying the store, Bruvi achieved a 30% increase in subscription sign-ups compared to their previous platform. Customer retention improved due to the seamless reorder system, and the site's modern design reinforced Bruvi's position as a premium, innovative coffee brand. The interactive product comparison tool helped customers make informed purchasing decisions, reducing return rates and increasing average order value.
