Trendy Wear PK is built on a Shopify foundation using Liquid templating for dynamic content rendering. The store is designed for the Pakistani market with local shipping (200 PKR across all Pakistan). HTML/CSS and JavaScript power the responsive layout and interactive elements, while Shopify Admin API handles real-time inventory and collection sync for Kids Collection, 2PC Collection, 3 PC Collection, and Women's Essentials.

Trendy Wear PK is a Pakistani clothing brand offering kids' wear and women's essentials. I developed a Shopify store that highlights their product collections — Kids Collection, 2PC Collection, 3 PC Collection, and Women's Essentials — creating a simple shopping experience for local customers with standard shipping of 200 PKR across Pakistan.

The client needed an online presence to reach Pakistani customers looking for affordable and stylish clothing. I built a Shopify store from the ground up — including theme customization, product page setup, collection organization, responsive layout adjustments, and integration of essential features like local shipping settings and SEO optimization.

One key challenge was setting up the store with limited product inventory while planning for future expansion. I organized collections clearly and set up placeholder sections for "Coming Soon" items like the 2PC Collection. Additionally, ensuring the store loaded quickly on mobile devices was critical for the Pakistani market, so I optimized images and implemented responsive design techniques.

After deploying the store, Trendy Wear PK established a professional online presence aligned with their brand identity. The client can now easily manage collections, products, and shipping settings through the familiar Shopify admin panel. The store is positioned for growth as they add more products to their 2PC and 3PC collections in the future.
