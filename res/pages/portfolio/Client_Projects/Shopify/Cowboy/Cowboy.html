Cowboy

Pioneering direct-to-consumer electric bikes for urban mobility.

Project Overview

Cowboy is a pioneering direct-to-consumer electric bike brand redefining urban mobility. I contributed to their Shopify-powered online store, focusing on a smooth, app-like browsing experience for their iconic bikes, parts, accessories, and test-ride bookings, with an emphasis on clean performance and modern aesthetics.

Visit Website

Your Role

I was responsible for front-end development, performance optimization, and checkout customization. I collaborated with the design team to implement a headless-like experience within Shopify's ecosystem, ensuring fast load times and smooth animations that mirror the feel of riding a Cowboy bike.

Features

  • High-performance Shopify store with app-like interactions
  • Integrated test-ride booking system with local dealer mapping
  • Custom bike configurator for color and accessory selection
  • Seamless checkout with financing options (Klarna, Affirm)
  • Dynamic product comparison tool
  • Optimized media delivery for bike videos and 360° product views

Tech Stack

Shopify Liquid Alpine.js Tailwind CSS GraphQL Shopify Storefront API

Challenges & Solutions

A major challenge was delivering a rich, interactive experience (bike configurator, 360° views) without slowing down the site. I implemented lazy loading for media assets and used Shopify's Storefront API with GraphQL to fetch only necessary data. The test-ride booking integration required custom liquid components to sync with their dealer management system — solved with webhooks and efficient state handling.

Results & Impact

The optimized store achieved significantly faster load times, resulting in lower bounce rates and higher conversion. Test-ride bookings increased by 25% after launch, and the smooth, modern design reinforced Cowboy's reputation as a premium, tech-forward e-bike brand.

Future Improvements

Planned updates include augmented reality (AR) bike preview in customer's environment, enhanced analytics for funnel optimization, and integration with Cowboy's companion app for seamless ownership experience.
