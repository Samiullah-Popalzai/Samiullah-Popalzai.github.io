Educación Global is a WordPress-based educational platform focused on international certification, institutional accreditation, and academic collaboration between global education centers. The goal was to present a professional, trustworthy, and structured digital presence that would appeal to institutional partners and certification seekers alike.

The client needed a platform that could clearly communicate complex accreditation information while maintaining a clean, credible aesthetic. I built a responsive WordPress layout using Elementor, with structured sections for About, Certification, Partners, and Accreditation content. The design emphasizes visual hierarchy, generous spacing, and trust-focused UI elements to enhance institutional credibility.

The main challenge was structuring complex institutional content in a way that remains clear and trustworthy. I solved this by implementing a strong visual hierarchy, spacing system, and section-based layout to improve readability and engagement. Each content block was carefully organized to guide visitors through the platform's offerings without overwhelming them.

After the redesign, Educación Global achieved improved clarity, institutional trust, and content accessibility. The platform now effectively presents itself as a structured international education and certification network. Visitors can easily find information about certification programs, accreditation processes, and partner institutions, leading to better engagement and inquiry conversion rates.
