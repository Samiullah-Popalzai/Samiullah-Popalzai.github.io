Wesley Mod Store is a WordPress e-commerce platform built for selling digital products and game mods. The focus was on creating a clean, fast, and conversion-optimized storefront with smooth navigation, product clarity, and a modern gaming-oriented design that appeals to the modding community.

The client needed a robust e-commerce solution that could handle digital product deliveries efficiently while maintaining a sleek, modern aesthetic. I built a WordPress-based storefront with WooCommerce integration, focusing on product layout, responsive design, and improving the overall user experience for digital product sales.

The main challenge was structuring a clean digital product store that remains fast and easy to navigate. I optimized layout hierarchy, reduced clutter in product pages, and improved mobile responsiveness for better conversions. I implemented a streamlined checkout process for digital goods, ensuring customers receive their mods instantly after purchase.

After deployment, the redesigned store improved usability and product visibility, resulting in a smoother customer journey and better engagement across product pages. The store now loads quickly, presents product information clearly, and provides a seamless digital delivery experience that keeps customers returning for more mods.
