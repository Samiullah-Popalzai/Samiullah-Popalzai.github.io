TekDukan

Your online marketplace for electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products.

Project Overview

TekDukan is a full-featured e-commerce website designed to connect customers with a wide variety of products, from electronics to clothing and lifestyle goods. The platform is optimized for both desktop and mobile use, offering a seamless shopping experience with secure checkout and multiple payment methods.

Your Role

I designed the UI/UX for TekDukan, implemented product browsing, search, shopping cart, and checkout flow. I integrated Firebase for backend services, including authentication, product management, and order tracking. I also developed the website with responsive design to ensure compatibility across all devices.

Features

  • Easy browsing and product search with filtering
  • Secure login and account management
  • Shopping cart with quick add/remove functions
  • Multiple payment options (cards, wallets, COD)
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop
  • Order tracking and email notifications

Tech Stack

HTML5 & CSS3 JavaScript PHP Responsive Web Design E-commerce Integration

Challenges & Solutions

Real-time product inventory updates and handling large amounts of product data were major challenges. I addressed this using Firebase's real-time database and optimized UI for smooth browsing. Ensuring a modern, appealing look for an electronics and lifestyle platform required a careful choice of layout, colors, and animations.

Results & Impact

TekDukan became a trusted platform offering a variety of products with a fast, smooth shopping experience. It increased customer satisfaction and engagement, and its responsive design ensured accessibility on all devices.

Future Improvements

Future upgrades include adding AI-powered recommendations, expanding payment gateway options, and integrating a loyalty rewards system for customers.
