Tazaj

E-commerce website and mobile app for online shopping.

Project Overview

Tazaj is a complete e-commerce platform built as both a website and mobile application. It allows users to browse products, add them to cart, and securely complete purchases with multiple payment methods. The goal of Tazaj was to create a smooth online shopping experience, focusing on beauty and lifestyle products.

Your Role

I was responsible for designing the UI/UX, implementing product listings, shopping cart, checkout flow, and integrating Firebase as the backend. I also developed both the website (responsive) and the Android mobile app using Flutter and Android Studio.

Features

  • Browse and search products with categories and filters
  • Secure user authentication and account management
  • Shopping cart and seamless checkout process
  • Payment gateway integration (credit/debit, wallets, etc.)
  • Responsive web design & cross-platform mobile app
  • Order tracking and notifications

Tech Stack

Flutter Firebase Android Studio Web Development E-commerce Integration

Challenges & Solutions

One of the main challenges was ensuring real-time synchronization of product inventory across website and app. I solved this by using Firebase as the single source of truth. Another challenge was building a design that felt modern and engaging for beauty products, which I achieved by using clean layouts, high-quality images, and smooth UI animations.

Results & Impact

Tazaj provided a smooth and secure shopping experience for users, increasing customer engagement and trust. The platform demonstrated strong performance across both web and mobile, making it a reliable solution for e-commerce businesses targeting the beauty and lifestyle industry.

Future Improvements

Planned upgrades include adding AI-powered product recommendations, integrating more local payment gateways, and improving analytics for business owners to track sales and customer behavior.
