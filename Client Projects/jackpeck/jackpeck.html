Jackpeck

A delightful memory game! Match the elemental icons.

Project Overview

Embark on a charming adventure with Jackpeck, the memory game that's as fun to say as it is to play! A whimsical grid of hidden cards awaits, each concealing a powerful elemental icon. Your mission is to use your sharp memory and quick wits to find and match the identical pairs.

With every successful match, you'll earn points and get closer to clearing the board. But watch the clock and keep an eye on your moves! This delightful brain-teaser features stylish 3D animations, a clean and colorful interface, and keeps track of all your best scores. Can you conquer the grid and achieve a perfect game? Download Jackpeck and start your delightful matching quest today.

Your Role

I was the sole developer for this project, responsible for designing the gameplay, coding the mechanics, creating the visuals, and optimizing performance across devices.

Features

  • Engaging memory gameplay with elemental icons
  • Stylish 3D animations and colorful interface
  • Score tracking for all your best matches
  • Challenging timed modes to test memory and speed
  • Optimized for desktop and mobile devices

Tech Stack

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Unity Engine C# Responsive Design

Challenges & Solutions

One major challenge was designing a visually appealing yet performant grid for multiple devices. I solved this by optimizing card animations and preloading assets efficiently. Balancing difficulty while keeping the game fun required iterative testing and adjustments to the matching logic.

Results & Impact

Jackpeck was well-received in playtesting, providing a fun, brain-teasing experience for players. It strengthened my skills in game logic, UI/UX design, and performance optimization, and demonstrated my ability to deliver a polished, engaging game.

Future Improvements

Future plans include adding multiple themes, additional game modes, multiplayer challenges, and daily rewards to enhance player engagement and replayability.
